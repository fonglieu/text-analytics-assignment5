About the Team:
The Performance Optimization Squad is a newly formed team in the Core Infrastructure Studio with a mission to establish a core competency in performance engineering and address systemic inefficiencies across Spotify's platform. With 3,200+ microservices, 40,000 VMs at peak, and 500,000 K8s pods, even minor fleet-wide efficiency improvements result in substantial cost savings. We've already identified $8M+ in annual savings from our first initiative alone.

About the Role:
We're looking for a Data Analyst who will build the measurement foundation that drives every decision we make. You'll build the data foundation from scratch—designing and owning the datasets, pipelines, and metrics that make performance inefficiencies visible across the platform.

Responsibilities:
- Design and own datasets, pipelines, and metrics that expose performance inefficiencies across the platform
- Write clean, efficient SQL and Python to model data and build lightweight pipelines
- Collaborate with engineers and product leads to define KPIs for infrastructure performance
- Build dashboards and reports that communicate optimization opportunities to stakeholders
- Perform exploratory analyses to uncover insights from large-scale infrastructure and cloud cost data
- Work with Kubernetes metrics, cost attribution, utilization signals, and observability data

Qualifications / Requirements:
- 2+ years of experience as a data analyst in a technical environment
- Strong SQL skills — comfortable writing complex queries from scratch
- Proficiency in Python for data manipulation, pipeline creation, and automation
- Experience with infrastructure, platform, or cloud cost data (Kubernetes metrics, cost attribution, GKE, JVM, resource utilization)
- Self-directed: able to identify problems in data independently
- Strong communication skills to present technical findings to non-technical partners

Preferred Skills:
- Familiarity with distributed systems and cloud infrastructure concepts
- Experience with dbt, Airflow, or similar pipeline tools
- Exposure to observability tooling (Datadog, Grafana, etc.)
- Prior experience at a high-growth technology company
