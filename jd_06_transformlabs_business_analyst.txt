About Transform Labs:
Transform Labs specializes in building innovative software solutions for diverse sectors, including healthcare, manufacturing, startups, and non-profit organizations. With 31 years of experience in software development, we offer our clients a fully-staffed Agile team with a proven track record of successful deliverables.

About the Role:
Are you a detail-oriented problem solver with a knack for understanding business goals and tech solutions? We're looking for a Business Analyst who's ready to dive into software development and help bridge the gap between our clients' needs and our engineering team's expertise. No previous experience as a Business Analyst required. We're looking for solid crossover skills, like communication, organization, curiosity, and a love of tech.

Employment Type: 1099 hourly, part-time (20 hours/average)
Pay: $42/hr
Location: Remote

Key Responsibilities:
- Work closely with clients to elicit requirements, understand their needs, and translate them into clear, actionable steps for our software engineers
- Collaborate with developers to ensure they fully understand project goals, constraints, and user expectations
- Develop and refine functional specifications, use cases, and flow diagrams, ensuring everyone is on the same page
- Keep an eye on project progress and communicate updates with clients and internal teams
- Help troubleshoot and resolve any project hiccups that might come up along the way
- Serve as liaison between the client and the development team to ensure smooth communication and alignment throughout the project lifecycle
- Gather feedback post-launch to keep improving our processes and outcomes

Qualifications / Requirements:
- No previous experience as a Business Analyst required — will train
- Great communication skills to bridge technical and non-technical stakeholders
- Strong problem-solving skills — able to dig into issues and come up with workable solutions
- A general understanding of software development processes
- Experience with tools like JIRA, Confluence, and any data visualization software is a plus
- Must be US-based; no OPT or sponsorship

Preferred Skills:
- Exposure to Agile methodologies
- Familiarity with data visualization tools (Tableau, Power BI, etc.)
- Experience in requirements documentation or business process mapping
