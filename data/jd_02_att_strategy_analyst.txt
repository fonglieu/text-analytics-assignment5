--- JOB DESCRIPTION ---

Job Overview:
You'll play a critical role in advancing AT&T's Wireline Transformation and Supply Chain (WTSC) Strategy Team. As a Strategy Analyst, you'll support collaborative problem-solving and provide analytical expertise to help shape billions of dollars in investments, drive cost transformation, and implement best-in-class practices across AT&T's expansive supply chain and networks. If you're motivated by data-driven problem-solving and thrive in a fast-paced, results-oriented environment, this is the opportunity for you.

Responsibilities:
- Analyze complex internal business problems and develop actionable solutions to drive business transformation
- Gather and standardize financial and operational data from multiple sources to uncover trends and improve cost efficiency
- Build and apply statistical and financial models to identify cost-saving opportunities, utilizing clean sheet and should-cost methodologies
- Develop and maintain dashboards and reports to track KPIs and inform executive decision-making
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams across supply chain, finance, and operations
- Prepare clear, compelling presentations and memos for senior leadership
- Support strategic planning cycles, including annual and quarterly technology and operations roadmaps
- Conduct market and competitive research to support strategic recommendations

Qualifications / Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Business, Finance, Economics, Engineering, or a related quantitative field
- 1-3 years of experience in analytics, consulting, finance, or a strategy-related role
- Strong proficiency in Microsoft Excel and PowerPoint
- Experience with SQL for data querying and analysis
- Demonstrated ability to work with large datasets and translate findings into actionable insights
- Strong written and verbal communication skills
- Ability to manage multiple priorities and deliver results in a fast-paced environment

Preferred Skills:
- Experience with data visualization tools such as Tableau or Power BI
- Familiarity with Python or R for data analysis
- Background in supply chain, logistics, or telecommunications
- Experience with financial modeling and cost analysis

Compensation:
$71,900 – $107,900 annually (Dallas, TX)
