About Goldman Sachs:
The Goldman Sachs Group, Inc. is a leading global investment banking, securities, and investment management firm that provides a wide range of financial services to a substantial and diversified client base, including corporations, financial institutions, governments, and high net worth individuals.

Business Overview:
The External Investing Group (XIG) provides investors with investment and advisory solutions, across leading hedge fund managers, private equity funds, real estate managers, public equity strategies, and fixed income strategies. The XIG Group manages globally diversified programs, targeted sector-specific strategies, customized portfolios, and a range of advisory services. With over 300 professionals across eight offices worldwide, XIG provides manager diligence, portfolio construction, risk management, and liquidity solutions — investing or advising on over $150 billion of alternative investments.

The XIG Data Office is broadly responsible for driving day-to-day governance and performance oversight of XIG's Private Markets data environment, through daily platform health and quality monitoring, proactively identifying structural design limitations, and partnering with XIG stakeholders to design, test, and implement next-generation data solutions.

About the Role:
The XIG Data Office is seeking a motivated Analyst to support data governance, reporting, and data quality initiatives. This role is well suited for an early career professional with data experience within private markets. The Analyst will work closely with senior Data Office team members to execute data initiatives, own uplift workstreams, support KPI monitoring, and contribute to improving data quality and operational efficiency across the XIG platform.

Employment Type: Full-time
Location: Salt Lake City, UT

Responsibilities – Data Operations & Reporting Support:
- Support daily monitoring of platform and vertical-specific KPIs to identify data quality issues and trends
- Assist in preparing dashboards, reports, and data summaries for internal stakeholders
- Help document data flows, definitions, and ownership across key datasets
- Perform data validation and reconciliation checks to support data accuracy and completeness

Responsibilities – Data Governance & Quality:
- Assist with implementing data quality metrics (integrity, completeness, accuracy, timeliness)
- Support remediation of data exceptions by working with functional teams and following established governance processes
- Contribute to documentation and controls that support data governance standards
- Apply best practices that reduce manual data entry and operational risk

Responsibilities – Project & Program Support:
- Support delivery of data initiatives by tracking actions, timelines, and dependencies
- Assist senior team members with data rationalization efforts, including mapping inputs, outputs, and data owners
- Contribute to testing and validation of enhancements to data platforms and reporting tools
- Participate in initiatives exploring automation and AI-enabled data solutions

Responsibilities – Stakeholder Engagement:
- Work with investment professionals, operations, legal, compliance, and technology partners to understand data needs
- Support preparation of materials for regular data status and progress updates
- Build foundational stakeholder management skills through exposure to cross-functional teams globally

Basic Qualifications:
- Bachelor's degree in Finance, Economics, Computer Science, Information Systems, or a related field
- 1–3 years of experience in data analysis, operations, technology, or a related role within financial services
- Strong analytical skills with attention to detail and data accuracy
- Proficiency in Excel; exposure to SQL, Python, or BI tools is a plus

Preferred Qualifications:
- Interest in private markets, alternative investments, or asset management
- Familiarity with data governance concepts or data quality frameworks
- Exposure to data warehousing, reporting platforms, or workflow tools
- Curiosity about automation, AI, or data-driven process improvement

Key Skills & Attributes:
- Strong organizational skills and ability to manage multiple tasks
- Clear written and verbal communication skills
- Willingness to learn in a fast-paced, collaborative environment
- Proactive mindset with an interest in building long-term data and investment management expertise
