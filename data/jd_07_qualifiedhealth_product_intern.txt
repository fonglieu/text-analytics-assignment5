About Qualified Health:
At Qualified Health, we're redefining what's possible with Generative AI in healthcare. Our infrastructure provides the guardrails for safe AI governance, healthcare-specific agent creation, and real-time algorithm monitoring — working alongside leading health systems to drive real change.

About the Role:
We are looking for a Product Intern to support our Product team in discovering, designing, and testing custom AI assistant solutions for healthcare clients. This role is ideal for a student or recent graduate with strong analytical skills, technical curiosity, and an interest in generative AI and healthcare technology. You will work closely with Product Leads and engineers to support product discovery, analyze workflows, and help test and evaluate AI-powered tools used by hospital systems.

Employment Type: Internship, Full-time
Pay: $40/hr
Location: Hybrid – Palo Alto, CA (3 days/week on-site)

Key Responsibilities:
- Support product discovery by helping document workflows, user needs, and product requirements
- Assist in identifying high-value use cases for AI assistants within healthcare systems
- Help configure, test, and evaluate AI assistants using internal tools and platforms
- Assist with gathering feedback from users and internal stakeholders
- Document findings, product insights, and potential improvements
- Collaborate with product managers, engineers, and cross-functional teams on product initiatives

- Interest in product management, AI, or healthcare technology
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills
- Excellent communication and organizational skills
- Curiosity, attention to detail, and eagerness to learn in a fast-paced startup environment
