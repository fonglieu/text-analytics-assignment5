About Delta Air Lines:
Delta Air Lines serves nearly 180 million customers each year. With an industry-leading global network, Delta and the Delta Connection carriers offer service to 330 destinations in 61 countries on six continents. Headquartered in Atlanta, Delta employs nearly 80,000 employees worldwide.

About the Role:
The Customer Data Solutions team is responsible for delivering powerful, insightful metrics, dashboards, and reports to support Delta's customer-facing operations. We are looking for an analytical, detail-oriented Senior Analyst to work with our existing data layer and help build scalable solutions that drive business decisions.

Responsibilities:
- Analyze large datasets related to customer behavior, scheduling, performance, and operational metrics
- Develop, maintain, and improve reporting tools, dashboards, and visualizations using Tableau and Python
- Write SQL queries from scratch to extract, model, and transform data from Teradata, SQL Server, and AWS environments
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams to identify data sources, perform data engineering, and maintain ETL pipelines
- Wrangle large datasets using Python and SQL to identify opportunities and understand performance trends
- Build and handle multi-source datasets to support department initiatives in a timely manner
- Leverage emerging technologies to efficiently communicate data and analysis to organizational leaders
- Develop and improve automated reports and analyses to support leadership and customer-facing teams
- Communicate complex data findings clearly to non-technical stakeholders

Qualifications / Requirements:
- Bachelor's degree in Data Science, Business Analytics, Statistics, Computer Science, or a related field
- 1+ years of significant work experience in a Business Analysis, Data Analytics, or Data Creation role
- Proficiency in data querying and modeling; ability to write SQL queries from scratch
- Experience with a scripting language to automate queries (Python preferred)
- Familiarity with data visualization tools: Tableau, Excel, Power BI
- Strong analytical and problem-solving skills to identify trends and patterns in data
- Ability to communicate complex data insights clearly and effectively to non-technical audiences
- Ability to work in a fast-paced, team environment with minimal direction

Preferred Skills:
- Advanced SQL modeling and querying skills
- Experience with AWS or cloud-based data platforms
- Familiarity with Alteryx, SAS, or similar data prep tools
- Master's degree or relevant certifications (e.g., Tableau Certification, Python for Data Science)
- Experience in the aviation, logistics, or transportation industry

Benefits:
- Travel privileges on Delta flights for employees and eligible family members
- Comprehensive health and wellness benefits
- 401(k) retirement savings plan with company matching
- Opportunities for professional development through training and career advancement
